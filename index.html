Omen

"A phantom of a memory, Omen hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold as his foe scrambles to learn where he might strike next. — Official description"

Omen is an Agent in VALORANT and one of the first Controllers available to play since the Closed Beta.
